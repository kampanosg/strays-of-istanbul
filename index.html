I visited Istanbul in April 2023. It has been a trip that I always wanted to do. When the opportunity came up, I went for it without any hesitations.

However, I almost immediately noticed a lot of strays, particularly cats. Thankfully, for the most part, locals look after them by leaving food - loads of chonky cats around! So while my friends were taking photos of the mosques and vast markets, I decided to capture the life of the strays of Istanbul.
