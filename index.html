I visited Istanbul in April 2023. It has been a trip that I always wanted to do and when the opportunity came up, I went for it without any hesitations.

And I am glad that I did! It is an amazing city, full of history, culture and amazing food.

However, I almost immediately noticed the large number of strays, and particularly cats. Thankfully, for the most part, locals seem to take care of them by leaving food - loads of chonky cats around! So while my friends were taking photos of the mosques and vast markets, I was capturing the life of the strays of Istanbul.
